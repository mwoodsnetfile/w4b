Friend's Triathlon

Best. Triathlon. Ever?

Possibly

One thought spurred on an entire movement: WHY NOT?. And thus the Friend's Triathlon was born. The thought was why spend $200 to compete in a highly organized triathlon where the competition is the only thing that matters and the pressure to perform perfectly is key. Race directors, JK Lundgren and Matt Woods decided they'd host a triathlon unlike any other - it had swimming, cycling, and running (though it is arguably bike-heavy), but it added what the rest of the triathlon scene was missing: Friendship. The rules were simple: invite friends and it must take place with no more than a week's notice.
